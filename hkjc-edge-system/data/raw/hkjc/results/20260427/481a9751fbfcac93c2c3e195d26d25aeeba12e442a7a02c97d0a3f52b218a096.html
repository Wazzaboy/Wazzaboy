Racing Incident Report

Pla. Horse No. Horse Incident
1 13 PACKING KING (K570) Bumped shortly after the start. Bumped near the 200 Metres. Sent for sampling post-race.
2 9 LUCRATIVE EIGHT (K542) Noted to have sustained a small wound to the right hind leg after the race. Sent for sampling post-race.
3 10 NAVAS G (L194) When questioned regarding her riding in the early stages of the Home Straight and the reason LUCRATIVE EIGHT was able to obtain clear running to her inside Apprentice H Y Yuen stated that making the Home Turn she improved to the outside of I EXCELLE but then her mount, which was having its first race start, shifted out further than she had anticipated. She said as a result LUCRATIVE EIGHT was able to obtain clear running to her inside near the 400 Metres. Apprentice Yuen was advised that whilst accepting her inexperience of riding in Hong Kong, wherever possible, she should make a greater effort to ride her mounts in a more competitive manner.
4 1 MATZDEN (L133) Jumped only fairly. Near the 200 Metres made contact with PACKING KING when both runners shifted ground slightly.
5 5 THE HEIR (H459) No report.
6 7 GUTSY BRAVO (L178) Jumped only fairly. From a wide barrier was shifted across behind runners in the early stages.
7 2 A TIME FOR US (K475) No report.
8 11 PRESTIGE HALL (K040) Jumped only fairly. Near the 1000 Metres was steadied to avoid THE HEIR when being shifted in behind that runner. For some distance near the 300 Metres had difficulty obtaining clear running when endeavouring to improve between PACKING KING and A TIME FOR US. H Bowman stated that once his mount obtained clear running it finished the race off only fairly in the Home Straight. Sent for sampling post-race.
9 3 AKERMANIS GOLD (L031) From the outside barrier was shifted across behind runners in the early stages. When commencing to finish the race off well was eased and shifted to the outside of LUCKY TWENTY near the 150 Meters when LUCKY TWENTY was giving ground.
10 12 BEAUTY GEMINI (L129) Jumped awkwardly. Raced keenly in the early stages and near the 950 Metres got its head up when being steadied. A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.
11 4 LUCKY TWENTY (K392) Bumped shortly after the start. A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.
12 8 HIGH RISE VICTORY (L128) No report.
13 14 I EXCELLE (K502) No report.
14 6 BRILLIANT WINNER (L203) After being placed in the starting gates became fractious, was removed and examined by the Veterinary Officer and passed suitable to race. After again being placed in the starting gates the start was delayed when the bridle on BRILLIANT WINNER was refitted. Before being allowed to race again, BRILLIANT WINNER will be required to perform satisfactorily in a stalls test. Jumped awkwardly. Shortly after the start became unbalanced when crowded between PACKING KING and LUCKY TWENTY, both of which shifted ground. Raced wide and without cover for the majority of the event.
